Salesforce Training

  • Asset: Active instance of Healthshare.
    1. Assets cannot be easily copied
    2. Assets are linked to the account.
    3. Assets are NOT memberassets.
  • Single moms need to either have their husband join their plan or there is a case for maternity.
  • The Rate Change changes all the product fields.
  • The total monthly contribution field should be the one source of truth in terms of member payment.
  • The payer is a separate field. Groups or children/adult children may have a contribution of 0.
  • Chargent charges the card.
    1. Chargent is the middleman between the processor and the gateway (NMI).
    2. The worker's aid uses a DIFFERENT gateway.
  • Standard, Complete, and Catastrophic are the primary plan types. Worker's Aid is a separate program to assist workers in the case of injury.

AHS Process Overview

VERY IMPORTANT: Always remember Social-Security-Exempt, Pre-Existing Conditions, and Alternative Treatment.

  • The goal is NOT to have people that are social security exempt. The older members of the company are more likely to be SSE.
  • There are multiple categories of leads in Salesforce, including new, follow up, holding, and ineligible.
  • A lead is created, then it becomes an opportunity, which becomes an account.
  • ALL INCOMING ACCOUNTS MUST BE CALLED BY THE SECRETARIES!!!!!!
  • We have set up some validation rules, which once confirmed, the lead can be converted to an opportunity.
  • The opportunity creates the account (you cannot go directly to the account).
    1. Ineligible or closed lost means you didn't join.
    2. If you are confirmed, you become closed won.
    3. A software flow will now create your account, your spouse and dependent accounts and all assets.
  • To open a case, the member must call AHS. Through the phone, the secretaries will transfer to the billing team. AHS makes the case. The billing team will include the hospital, how long the member was there, if the condition was pre-existing, exc.
  • As billing manager, Keny is assigned all cases.
  • Cases are linked to a person's asset so we can report on how much was lost in cases.
  • Reoccurring cases use the previous case as a parent case and are restarted every year.
  • Customers email their bills to the billing team, who then uploads them to Salesforce.
  • The original bill = no discount. The submitted bill = discount.
  • The "not shareable" field is used to track patients that do not have a shareable bill but we still negotiate for them or there is a shared with hardship fund where the bill is paid anyway.
  • The ISA is the bills that are recieved from the hospital. We are supposed to reimburse them for that? We remiburse the amount charged.
  • Opera Documents is the platform for generating welcome letters and billing documents.
  • Cases cannot be closed until the someone pays.
  • Keny, then Jackson, then Rosalyn are the defaults for starting a billing case.
  • All employees have a Teams phone extension.
  • The accountants use QuickBooks for billing. Salesforce and Quickbooks are not automatically integrated.
